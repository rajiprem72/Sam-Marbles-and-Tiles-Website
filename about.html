About Us

Building Trust Through Quality and Commitment

Dr. C. M. Victor Emmanuel

Dr. Victor Emmanuel devoted several years of his life to serving society as a medical professional, caring for thousands of patients with compassion, dedication and a deep sense of responsibility. His commitment to improving lives earned him the trust and respect of countless families.

Driven by his entrepreneurial spirit and a strong desire to continue his family’s legacy, he later joined Sam Marbles & Granites. Bringing the same sincerity, discipline and customer-first approach from his medical career, he has successfully transformed the showroom into one of Chennai’s trusted destinations for premium marbles, granites and natural stone solutions.

Under his leadership, the company continues to uphold its core values of quality, transparency and customer satisfaction, helping homeowners, architects and builders create beautiful and long-lasting spaces.

What We Offer

True to our name, Sam Marbles & Granites offers a diverse collection of premium marble, granite and natural stone products. Carefully sourced from leading suppliers across India, our collection is designed to suit residential, commercial and architectural projects of every scale.

Over the years, our company has grown steadily with a single objective: to meet the infrastructure and interior requirements of our customers through a well-organized process of cutting, polishing and finishing using the latest technologies available in the industry.
