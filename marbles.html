Premium Marble Collection

Timeless Elegance Crafted in Natural Stone

Marble has been admired for centuries for its beauty, strength and luxurious appearance. Its unique textures, natural patterns and rich colours make it one of the most preferred materials for homes, commercial spaces and architectural projects.

At Sam Marbles & Granites, we offer a carefully selected range of premium marble varieties sourced from leading suppliers across India. Whether you are designing flooring, walls, staircases, countertops or interiors, our collection combines elegance, durability and timeless appeal.

Our Marble Collection

We at Sam Marbles & Granites currently deal with the following premium marble varieties.

Indian Pegasus White

Indian Pegasus White marble is admired for its elegant white surface and subtle natural patterns. Its sophisticated appearance makes it an ideal choice for flooring, walls and premium interior spaces. The marble blends beautifully with both modern and traditional designs.

Wonder White Marble

Wonder White Marble is known for its bright finish and graceful texture. It enhances the beauty of living spaces with its clean and luxurious appearance. Its durability makes it suitable for residential and commercial projects.

Dharmeta Marble

Dharmeta Marble offers a distinctive natural pattern that adds charm to interiors. It is widely used for flooring, wall cladding and decorative applications. Its timeless beauty continues to make it a popular choice among homeowners and architects.

Morwad White Marble

Morwad White Marble is valued for its smooth texture and elegant white appearance. It creates a bright and spacious ambience in homes and commercial spaces. The marble is easy to maintain and highly durable.

Katni Marble

Katni Marble is famous for its natural shades and attractive patterns. It offers a classic appearance that complements a variety of architectural styles. Its strength and elegance make it suitable for long-term use.

Wonder Marble

Wonder Marble brings a luxurious touch to interiors with its refined finish. It is widely used in premium homes, hotels and office spaces. Its natural beauty enhances the overall aesthetics of any environment.

Sawar Marble

Sawar Marble is appreciated for its elegant texture and long-lasting quality. It is commonly used for flooring and decorative applications. Its natural finish gives spaces a sophisticated look.

Raymond White

Raymond White marble features a graceful combination of colour and texture. It creates a premium appearance in both residential and commercial projects. The marble is known for its durability and timeless elegance.

Pink Marble

Pink Marble adds warmth and character to any space with its attractive shades. Its unique appearance makes it suitable for decorative interiors and feature walls. It offers both beauty and durability.

Green Marble

Green Marble stands out for its rich colour and striking natural patterns. It is frequently used to create luxurious interiors and statement spaces. Its unique appearance adds a touch of elegance to any project.

Green Marble Lapotra

Green Marble Lapotra features a polished finish that enhances its natural beauty. It is suitable for flooring, countertops and decorative applications. Its premium look makes it a preferred choice for sophisticated interiors.
